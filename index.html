New age of your design process.

Colure is a new mobile application that generates color palette based on your project needs. All you need is describe your project, identify target audience and choose type of project and your color palette is just a tap away! Colure is always accurate and it understands you ot your client. You wouldn't be dissapointed.

Features

Saves time

The innovative algorithm helps you save lots of time spend on research and deep understanding of the topic. By just typing in a small amount of information, in just a few minutes you are getting a full and sophisticated summary based on millions of websites and colors databases.

Knows you

The application has a unique analysis system. Based on the data given by the user it returns several accurate and case specific results. Each color set has a complete description and several example links.

Reports it

The system creates a full report for the chosen color set. This report is fully customizable with the "template editor" tool. The report system is multilingual and supports 6 languages.
